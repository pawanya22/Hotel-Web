Who we are

Luxury Travel Guru is a premium travel brand dedicated to creating exceptional journeys across Sri Lanka, the Maldives, and selected South Asian destinations. As a proud extension of Ceylon Travel Dream Private Limited, we bring years of expertise, trusted partnerships, and a deep passion for travel into every experience we design. Our focus is simple – to deliver seamless, elegant, and personalized travel experiences that reflect true comfort and style.

We specialize in tailor-made luxury holidays, carefully crafted to match each traveler’s unique preferences. From handpicked beachfront villas in the Maldives to exclusive boutique stays and cultural escapes in Sri Lanka, every detail is thoughtfully arranged. With strong connections to leading hotels and resorts, we ensure access to the finest properties while offering highly competitive direct-to-client rates. Whether it’s a relaxing escape, a romantic getaway, or a private family journey, we design each itinerary with precision and care.

At Luxury Travel Guru, service goes beyond planning. Our dedicated team is available 24/7 to assist you at every stage of your journey, ensuring peace of mind from arrival to departure. With private transfers, personalized concierge services, and a commitment to excellence, we turn every trip into a smooth and memorable experience. Your comfort, trust, and satisfaction are at the heart of everything we do.
